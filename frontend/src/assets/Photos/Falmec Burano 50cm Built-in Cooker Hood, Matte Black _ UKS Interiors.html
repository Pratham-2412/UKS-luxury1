Shipping Policy

At UKS Interiors, we aim to provide a straightforward and efficient collection process for all our customers. Please review our shipping policy to understand how we handle your orders and the options available for collecting your purchases.

1. Collection Only

We currently do not offer delivery services. All orders placed through UKS Interiors are available for pick-up/collection only from our designated address.

2. Collection Address

  • UKS Interiors
  • 32 Victoria Road, Ruislip, HA4 0AB
  • Collection Hours:
  • Monday to Friday: 10:00 AM - 6:00 PM
  • Saturday: 10:00 AM - 4:00 PM
  • Closed on Sundays and Bank Holidays

3. Handling Time

  • Order Processing: Once you place an order, our team will process it within 1-2 working days. We will notify you via email or phone as soon as your order is ready for collection.
  • Ready for Collection: You will receive a notification when your order is ready for pick-up. Please wait for this confirmation before coming to our collection point.

4. Collection Requirements

  • Identification: Please bring a valid form of identification (e.g., driving licence, passport) and your order confirmation email when collecting your order.
  • Authorised Pick-Up: If someone else is collecting the order on your behalf, please ensure they bring a signed letter of authorisation and a copy of your ID.

5. Collection Restrictions

  • Same-Day Collection: Please note that we do not offer same-day collection. All orders require processing time before they are available for pick-up.
  • Delivery: We do not offer delivery services, including to P.O. boxes or remote locations. All purchases must be collected from our specified address.
  • International Orders: We currently only serve customers within the UK and do not facilitate international orders or collections.

6. Contact Information

For any questions or to arrange a collection, please contact our customer service team:

  • Email: sales@uks-interiors.com
  • Phone: 01895 347277


Returns Policy

At UKS Interiors, we are committed to ensuring you are completely satisfied with your purchase. We understand that sometimes a product may not meet your expectations, or you may simply change your mind. In line with UK Distance Selling Regulations, we have crafted our return policy to be fair, transparent, and easy to follow.


1. Your Right to Cancel

Under UK Distance Selling Regulations, you have the right to cancel your order within 14 days of receiving your goods. This period is known as the “cooling-off” period. You do not need to provide a reason for cancellation.

  • How to Cancel: To exercise your right to cancel, please notify us in writing via email at sales@uks-interiors.com or by post. Please include your order number, the item(s) you wish to return, and your contact details.

2. Returns Period

At UKS Interiors, we extend the standard cancellation period to 14 days from the date of delivery, during which you can return items for a refund.

  • Eligibility: To be eligible for a return, the item must be unused, in its original packaging, and in the same condition that you received it.

3. Return Process

To initiate a return, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact Us: Email sales@uks-interiors.com or call 01895 347277 to request a Return Authorisation Number (RAN) within 30 days of delivery. Provide your order number and details of the item(s) you wish to return.
  2. Return Authorisation: Once your return is authorised, we will provide you with a RAN and instructions on how to return your item.
  3. Packing Your Return: Securely pack your item in its original packaging, include any documentation, and clearly label the package with the RAN.
  4. Return Shipping: You are responsible for covering the return shipping costs. We recommend using a tracked and insured service, as UKS Interiors cannot be held liable for items lost or damaged in transit.
  5. Processing Your Return: Once we receive your returned item, we will inspect it and notify you via email within 10 working days. Refunds will be processed to your original payment method, and exchanges will be dispatched as soon as possible.

4. Refunds and Exchanges

  • Full Refunds: Eligible returns will receive a full refund, credited to your original payment method within 14 days of us receiving the returned item.
  • Exchanges: If you wish to exchange an item, please specify the replacement item when contacting us for a RAN. We will process the exchange once the original item is received.
  • Store Credit: Alternatively, you can choose to receive store credit, which will be applied to your account for future purchases.

5. Non-Returnable Items

Certain items are exempt from being returned:

  • Custom-Made Orders: Items that have been made to your specifications or personalised are non-returnable unless faulty.
  • Sale Items: Items purchased on sale or at a discount are final sale and cannot be returned.
  • Used or Damaged Items: Items that are not in their original condition, have been used, or are damaged after delivery cannot be returned.

6. Faulty or Incorrect Items

If you receive an item that is faulty, damaged, or incorrect, please contact us immediately. We will arrange for the item to be returned at our expense and offer you a full refund, replacement, or repair, as appropriate.

7. Return Shipping Costs

  • Standard Returns: You are responsible for the cost of returning non-faulty items. We do not provide prepaid labels.
  • Faulty/Incorrect Items: We will cover the return shipping costs for items that are faulty, damaged, or incorrectly sent.

8. Condition of Returned Items

To qualify for a refund or exchange, items must be returned in their original condition:

  • Packaging: Items should be returned in their original packaging with all tags, manuals, and accessories.
  • Condition: Items must be unused and undamaged.

9. Contact Information

For any questions or to start a return, please contact our customer service team:

  • Email: sales@uks-interiors.com
  • Phone: 01895 347277
  • Mail: UKS Interiors, 32 Victoria Road, Ruislip, HA4 0AB.

Please Note: This return policy does not affect your statutory rights under the Consumer Contracts Regulations 2013.
